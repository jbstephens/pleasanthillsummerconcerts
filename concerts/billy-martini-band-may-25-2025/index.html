About This Concert

The 2025 Sunset by the Lake Summer Concert Series kicks off with The Billy Martini Band! Known for their energetic performances and classic rock repertoire, they're the perfect way to start the summer concert season.

The Billy Martini Band brings high-energy performances featuring rock classics that will have you dancing all evening. Their dynamic stage presence and crowd-engaging performances make them a crowd favorite at venues throughout the Bay Area.

What to bring: Blankets, lawn chairs, and picnic baskets are encouraged. Food trucks and beverage vendors will be available on-site.

Parking: Free parking is available at Pleasant Hill City Hall and surrounding areas.
