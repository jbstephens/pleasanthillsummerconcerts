About This Concert

Dirty Cello returns to Pleasant Hill to captivate audiences with their unique blend of blues and bluegrass fusion!

Led by virtuoso cellist Rebecca Roudman, Dirty Cello brings an unforgettable musical experience that defies genre boundaries. Their performances blend the soulful sounds of blues with the foot-stomping energy of bluegrass, all centered around Rebecca's extraordinary cello playing.

With a reputation for electrifying live shows, Dirty Cello has performed everywhere from high-profile festivals to intimate venues, always leaving audiences wanting more.

What to bring: Blankets, lawn chairs, and picnic baskets are encouraged. Food trucks and beverage vendors will be available on-site.
